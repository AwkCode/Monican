# LinkedIn Message Templates — Real Estate Agency Outreach

## Connection Request Note (300 char limit)

### Version A: Local angle
Hi {first_name} — I'm based in Bolton and I help real estate teams automate admin work with AI. I'd love to connect and learn about how {agency_name} handles the ops side. No pitch, just curious!

### Version B: Value-forward
Hi {first_name} — I build AI tools that save real estate agents 10+ hrs/week on lead follow-up, listings, and transaction admin. Would love to connect and share what I'm seeing in the space.

### Version C: Warm/mutual connection
Hi {first_name} — saw we're both in the {town} area. I help small businesses automate repetitive work with AI and I've been focused on real estate lately. Would love to connect!

---

## Follow-Up Message (After connection accepted)

Thanks for connecting, {first_name}!

I've been helping real estate teams figure out where AI can actually save time — not the hype stuff, the practical stuff. Things like:

- Auto-drafting lead responses in seconds instead of hours
- Generating listing descriptions from property details
- Tracking transaction deadlines and flagging missing docs

I'm offering free workflow audits for a few local agencies this month. It's a 30-min call where I map out your team's biggest time sinks and show what could be automated.

No cost, no commitment. Would you be open to a quick conversation?

---

## USAGE NOTES

- Send connection request first, wait for acceptance, THEN send the follow-up
- Space out connection requests — 3-5 per day max to avoid LinkedIn restrictions
- Like/comment on their posts for a week before connecting if possible
- If they post about being busy, overwhelmed, or hiring → that's your opening
- Never send the full pitch in the connection request — it's too long and too salesy
