### LIKELY PAIN POINTS (Based on size and type)

For a {SIZE}-agent {TYPE} agency, the most common pain points are:

1. **Lead response time** — New leads from Zillow/Realtor.com/website sit in inbox for hours. Agents are busy showing homes and can't respond fast enough. Hot leads go cold.

2. **Listing description writing** — Every new listing needs MLS descriptions, marketing copy, social media posts. Takes 30-60 min per listing. Agents hate writing.

3. **Transaction coordination** — After a deal is accepted, there's a cascade of deadlines, documents, and follow-ups. Missed deadlines = blown deals. Often one person is the bottleneck.

4. **Email/inbox management** — Agents get 100+ emails/day. Sorting, prioritizing, and responding is a time black hole.

5. **CRM hygiene** — Contact records are messy, follow-up tasks fall through cracks, no one updates the CRM consistently.

3. "Who handles the admin after an offer is accepted? How do they track all the deadlines?"
4. "{CUSTOM QUESTION based on research}"

---

### WHAT TO LISTEN FOR

- **"We're drowning"** → They need automation NOW. Fast path to a sale.
- **"We tried [tool] but it didn't work"** → Ask why. Position yourself as the implementation expert.
- **"Our admin person handles that"** → Find out how many hours. That person's salary is your ROI lever.
- **"We use [CRM name]"** → Note it. Determines which integrations are possible.
- **"We're growing"** → Growth = more pain. Their current process won't scale. Use this.

### COMPETITION CHECK

- **Are they already using AI tools?** If yes, what tools? Are they happy?
- **Have they been pitched by other AI consultants?** What turned them off?
- **Are they tech-forward or tech-resistant?** Adjust your language accordingly.
