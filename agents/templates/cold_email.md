## PERSONALIZATION NOTES

When drafting for a specific agency, reference:
- Their location (especially if close to Bolton — use the local angle)
- Their size (solo agent vs team affects the pain points you lead with)
- Anything notable from their website (awards, specialties, recent growth)
- Their broker/owner's name and background if available
- Whether they're independent (more autonomy = easier to sell) vs franchise
