## Tips for LinkedIn Outreach

- Send connection requests in batches of 10-15 per day max so LinkedIn doesn't flag you
- Personalize at least one line if you can
- If they accept but don't respond, wait a day then send a follow up DM (can be longer than 300 chars)
- Follow up DM template:

"Hey [Name], thanks for connecting. Like I mentioned I'm just looking to have conversations with people in real estate to learn about the business. Would you be open to a quick 15 minute call or coffee sometime? No pitch, no agenda. Just want to hear your perspective. Thanks either way."
